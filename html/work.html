Bezier Surfaces

Rendering Bezier surfaces in OpenGL. The program tessellates input Bezier patches to form triangles. It then computes the exact Gaussian Curvature directly from the Weingarten map. Further it computes an approximation to the Gaussian Curvature from the triangles and their associated normals. Below you can see the comparison between the exact computation and the approximation. In the approximations there is some noticeable tearing on the surface.
